Chuyang Wu designs & builds digital infrastructure for humans. He believes that autonomy is earned, not assumed. That form is a function of function. That signal outranks spectacle. And that elegance is hierarchical and recursive.

He is inspired by a childhood steeped in both history and the avant-garde, shaped between Nanjing and New York City, and is now based in Shenzhen.

He is currently working on his agentic startup project. He has previously interned at Tencent and Miracleplus (YC China). He obtained his Bachelor’s of Arts at Cornell University in Computer Science and Economics, minoring in History and previously led teams on Cornell SDS.
